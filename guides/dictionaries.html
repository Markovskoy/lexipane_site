Offline dictionaries

Free FreeDict Local packs · fallback quality · language coverage

Offline packs are downloaded from FreeDict and stored in app data. Lexipane checks local packs before the online translator, so common words can work without AI and without sending a request.

Installed dictionary packs are shown in Settings.

Coverage

Current app languages are Russian, English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese and Vietnamese. FreeDict does not provide every pair.

  • Good coverage: English, Russian, German, French, Spanish, Italian and Portuguese pairs.
  • Partial coverage: Japanese has useful packs for English/German/French/Russian directions, but not every pair.
  • Very limited: Chinese has packs for a few directions only.
  • No FreeDict pack found: Korean and Vietnamese pairs in the current language list.

Asian languages

Japanese, Korean and Chinese need real word segmentation. LunaTranslator uses MeCab/UniDic for Japanese and Jieba/Pinyin for Chinese; those are good optional-pack candidates, but they are too heavy to bundle silently. Until an analyzer pack is added, use online translation or AI for best quality on Japanese and Korean.

Google fallback

When online mode is enabled, Lexipane can fall back to the Google Translate unofficial endpoint. Rate-limit protection is enabled to avoid temporary 429/403 blocks.
