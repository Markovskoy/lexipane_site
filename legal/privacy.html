Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

1. What data Lexipane collects

Lexipane is a local desktop application. It does not have its own servers and does not collect or transmit personal data on its own.

  • Screen content — text captured from your screen is processed locally on your device. It is not sent anywhere unless you explicitly enable a feature that requires an external service.
  • AI provider requests — if you configure a third-party AI provider (such as OpenAI) or use the built-in Pro AI credits, the captured text is sent to that provider's API. This is governed by that provider's own privacy policy.
  • Anki export — words and phrases you choose to save are sent to your local Anki installation via AnkiConnect. No data leaves your machine through this path.
  • Settings and history — stored locally on your device in application data files.

2. Third-party services

When you use an external AI provider, your captured text is subject to that provider's terms and privacy policy. Lexipane does not control how third-party providers process your data.

3. License and subscription data

When paid subscriptions launch, billing and license verification will be handled by a third-party payment provider. That provider's privacy policy will govern any data collected during the payment process.

4. No analytics or telemetry

Lexipane does not include any analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry that sends data to Lexipane or third parties without your knowledge.

5. Children

Lexipane is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as features change. The current version is always published at this URL.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy can be raised in the official Telegram channel.
